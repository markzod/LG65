Shawnigan Lake BnB: here are several convenient services close by:

- Small grocery/convenience/bakery (Masons), stores, bakeries, coffee shops, gas station, subway, sushi and restaurants: 3-5 minute drive at Shawnigan Lake village

- Bus stops: 5 minute walk
- Full services with 30+ stores at Mill Bay Centre: 10 minute drive

- There is a winery, a cideryand lakeside restaurant all 5-10 minutes drive

- Gallery Marina which has a gas dock, restaurant and board rentals accessible by boat and vehicle
