Shawnigan Lake BnB: a few extras that are all within 5 to 10 minutes drive:

- Mason’s store (bakery and general store)
- Merridale Cidery / restaurant
- Unsworth Vineyard
- Lakeside Restaurant
- Blackswan Pub and Liquor Store
- Gallery Marina
- Sarpinos Pizza and Pasta
- Hasuka sushi
- Subway
- Shawnigan House Coffee and Chocolate
- Spicecraft Flavours of India
- Montra Thai

There are several walking trails and adventure parks within 5-30 minutes drive:
- Masons Beach / trail
- kinsol trestle (recommend)
- Wildplay Nanaimo Adventure Park
- Malahat skywalk

NOTE: if you look up Mill Bay Plaza, which is about a 12 minute drive away there are about 30 more stores, including thrifty foods (a large, grocery store) shoppers pharmacy, BC liquor store, etc.
